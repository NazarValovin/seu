TIPS FOR YOUR FIRST DAY

We’re so glad you’re here! Browse this page for the official run down to our clubs. Don’t forget to claim your first timer special below with our exclusive, intro offer.
ARRIVE EARLY
Arrive 15 minutes early and check-in at front desk. We’ll give you a facility tour and knock out any paperwork.
MEET YOUR INSTRUCTOR
Say hello and let them know if you have any injuries, questions or concerns.
WATER
Water bottles are also for sale and we have water fountains.
PACE YOURSELF
Most classes are 60 minutes - Cycling is 45 minutes. Stay hydrated and don’t be afraid to take a break.
REPLENISH
Refuel your body. Seung-ni offers a variety of protein shakes and protein bars to help your body recover.
HAVE FUN
Meet your fellow members and participate in our events!
