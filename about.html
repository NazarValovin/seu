THE SEUNG-NI STYLE

FOUNDED BY

Gary Hausbeck founded Seung-ni in 1989. He started out as a martial art academy teaching Taekwondo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and evolved and added group fitness to the program in 1995. After only 2 years he formulated a weight loss program called, the “Seung-ni Body Challenge” that combines fitness, nutrition, and accountability. Since starting the group fitness program, Seung-ni has trained over 100,000 members and over 40,000 have participated the Body Challenge.

WHAT IS SEUNG-NI

At Seung-ni we want you to be part of our “fit fam”. Everyone is welcomed into our community. You’ll find support, motivation and guidance from people who are on the same journey as you. Whether that journey is to better your health and wellness or improve your strength and cardio, there will always be someone who has your back.

Our certified instructors will lead your through every workout and will help you achieve the greatest results in the shortest time. You’ll never repeat the same workout twice which means your muscles never get used to the same boring routine. Workouts never feel stale no matter how often you go! All you have to do is show up and follow along!

WHY SEUNG-NI

There are many gyms in the area that are fully equipped with everything a person needs to lose wight and get in great shape. However, most people that join these gyms don’t get the results they are looking for because of time or lack of motivation. What makes Seung-ni so impressive is our instructors will motivate you by and give you the best possible workout in 55 minutes or less. That means you’ll have more time and receive quicker results. Our culture strives to get to know our members and make everyone feel welcomed and comfortable. We hope to welcome you into our Seung-ni Fit Family!
